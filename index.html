What Hitchi Guarantee

Security

We want you to travel safely ! That's why we focus on transparency, driver's monitoring and rating. HitchiGo is built with the highest tech standards in order to provide you the safiest experience possible.

Quickness

Finding a Hitchhiker or a driver is as fast as ordering a coffee! Thanks to the 500 000 daily users it's super easy to find a HitchiGo user. Using HitchiGo, your travel experiences is going to be fast and flawless.

Fun

For HitchiGo, one of the main priority is to gamify hitchhiking experiences, that's why you have access to many features like leaderboards (eco-ranking), badges and rewards. Be ready to spread good vibes!
